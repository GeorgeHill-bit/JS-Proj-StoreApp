The Bicycle Garage

We furnish a complete line of bicycle brands to suit your needs and style at competitive prices. The Bicycle Garage also provides complete assembly of purchased bikes with customized fitting. And, because we like to have fun we also have regular activities such as group rides, instructional sessions on riding safety (especially focused on child safety) and important bicycle maintenance.
